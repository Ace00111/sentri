What is Sentri?

Sentri is an AI-powered crypto security copilot that helps users understand and safely interact with blockchain transactions before they sign them.

Instead of showing users confusing smart contract data, Sentri translates it into clear, human-readable explanations, detects potential risks, researches tokens, and provides AI-driven recommendations to help prevent scams and costly mistakes.


 By combining real-time transaction analysis with advanced threat detection, Sentri provides a bulletproof layer of security between your wallet and the blockchain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Sentri store my private keys? expand_more
Absolutely not. Sentri is non-custodial and operates as a simulation layer. Your keys stay on your device or hardware wallet.
Which blockchains are supported? expand_more
Currently, we support Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, and Arbitrum and others
Is there a mobile app? expand_more
Not yet. We're focused on delivering the best web experience first, with browser extension and mobile support coming soon.
